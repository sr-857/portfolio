# Brand Identity: Subhajit Roy

## 1. Tagline
**"Securing systems with code, intelligence, and automation."**

## 2. Mission Statement
"I help organizations detect, investigate, and mitigate cyber threats by combining hands-on security engineering, threat hunting, and automation. I build tools, playbooks, and platforms that turn raw logs and indicators into actionable defense."

## 3. Brand Keywords
- **Threat Hunting & Detection Engineering**
- **Security Automation & SIEM**
- **Malware Analysis & Forensics**
- **Cloud & Container Security**
- **Hackathon-Driven Innovation**

## 4. Target Roles
- SOC Analyst / Security Engineer
- Threat Hunter / Detection Engineer
- Cybersecurity Analyst (Blue Team)
- Junior Penetration Tester

## 5. Value Proposition
Subhajit bridges the gap between **offensive knowledge** (vulnerability assessment) and **defensive engineering** (building tools, automating SOC workflows). He is not just an analyst who looks at alerts; he is an engineer who builds the systems that generate and manage those alerts.
